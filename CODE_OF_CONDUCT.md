# **Code of Conduct for itsRIGHTtime and its Subsidiaries**

## **Introduction**

For simplicity, all references to **itsRIGHTtime** in this Code of Conduct include _its subsidiaries_ and _products_, such as **letsCREATE**, unless explicitly stated otherwise.

## **Our Pledge**

At **letsCREATE** (a product or subsidiary under **itsRIGHTtime**), we are committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive environment for all employees, contributors, and stakeholders involved with our products and subsidiaries. We pledge to create an experience free from harassment, discrimination, and unprofessional behavior, ensuring everyone feels valued and respected.

We commit to acting in ways that contribute to an open, inclusive, diverse, and supportive community both internally and in our interactions with customers, clients, and external partners.

## **Our Standards**

### **Examples of Positive Behavior**

- Demonstrating empathy, kindness, and respect toward others.
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences.
- Offering constructive feedback and being open to receiving it.
- Taking responsibility for actions and learning from mistakes.
- Prioritizing the best interests of the team and the organization.

### **Examples of Unacceptable Behavior**

- Using discriminatory, derogatory, or offensive language.
- Insulting, trolling, or making personal attacks.
- Public or private harassment of any kind.
- Sharing personal or sensitive information without consent.
- Engaging in behaviors that disrupt or harm the working environment.

## **Enforcement Responsibilities**

The management team at **itsRIGHTtime**, including team leaders and HR representatives, is responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct. Any behavior deemed unacceptable, threatening, or harmful will be addressed in accordance with internal policies and applicable labor laws and may result in corrective actions, ranging from warnings to termination.

Management reserves the right to remove or reject contributions, comments, or actions that conflict with this Code of Conduct and will document and provide timely, transparent communication regarding such decisions.

## **Scope**

This Code of Conduct applies within **itsRIGHTtime**, including all company projects, meetings, and communications, both in-person and virtual, involving employees, contractors, collaborators, and external partners. It also applies to interactions with clients or on social media when representing **itsRIGHTtime** or its subsidiaries.

## **Reporting and Enforcement**

### **How to Report Violations**

If you experience or witness any behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it to your manager or HR representative. Reports will be investigated promptly and treated with confidentiality.

For immediate concerns or formal reports, you can also contact HR at [hr@itsrighttime.group](mailto:hr@itsrighttime.group). Reports may be made anonymously through the companyâ€™s internal reporting system [Report Anonymously](https://www.itsrighttime.group/report).

### **Consequences for Violating the Code of Conduct**

#### **1. Warning**

- **Community Impact**: Inappropriate language or behavior that disrupts the work environment.
- **Consequence**: A private, written warning explaining the violation and expected behavior.

#### **2. Temporary Suspension**

- **Community Impact**: A series of inappropriate behaviors or more severe incidents.
- **Consequence**: A temporary suspension from company projects, meetings, or communications for a specified duration. A mandatory review with the individual, their manager, and HR will follow to determine corrective actions.

#### **3. Termination of Employment or Contract**

- **Community Impact**: A continued pattern of violations, harassment, or severe unprofessional behavior.
- **Consequence**: Immediate termination of employment or contract with **itsRIGHTtime**. Legal action may follow depending on the nature of the violation.

## **Commitment to Continuous Improvement**

**itsRIGHTtime** is dedicated to continuously improving our culture and practices. We encourage open dialogue about how we can create a better, more inclusive work environment. All employees are encouraged to bring forward suggestions, feedback, or concerns to promote the overall well-being of the organization and its products.
